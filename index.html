About Linggi Palace Resort

Welcome to Linggi Palace Resort, located at the heart of Roing, Arunachal Pradesh. We offer a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and authentic hospitality for both tourists and business travelers.

Nestled in the serene landscapes of Northeast India, our resort provides an ideal base for exploring the natural beauty and cultural richness of Roing, including the famous Mehao Lake and Mehao Wildlife Sanctuary.

Our Location

Roing, Arunachal Pradesh

Located at the heart of Roing township, our resort is the perfect base to explore the natural and cultural wonders of Northeast India.

Nearby Attractions

  • Mehao Lake: Beautiful alpine lake surrounded by stunning mountain scenery
  • Mehao Wildlife Sanctuary: Home to diverse flora and fauna
  • Local Villages: Experience authentic tribal culture and traditions
  • Roing Market: Traditional shopping and local delicacies
  • Adventure Activities: Trekking, nature walks, and exploration

Getting Here

• Nearest Airport: Dibrugarh (180 km) - 4 hours drive

• Nearest Railway: Naharlagun (200 km) - 4.5 hours drive

• Direct flights available from major Indian cities
